Foliage Pavilion

1 Dec 2014

Inspired by the beautiful phenomenon of sunlight shining through shifting leaves, the Foliage Pavilion model is designed to mimic foliageâ€™s play of light in its interior at all times of day.

The springs attached just below the panels allow the panels to bobble in the wind. The effect of springs combined with the disparity in hole size, hole arrangement, and panel arrangement allow for a more unpredictable light phenomenon based on sun and wind conditions. Circles of light shift, blink, and overlap as the sun shines through the naturally dynamic porous layers.
