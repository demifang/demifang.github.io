Variations on a quadratic function: dimensionality reduction

31 Aug 2020

Tasked with producing a design space of mathematical functions that are smooth, differentiable, and concave down on the x-y domain, a 9-dimensional variable space of transformations on a paraboloid is constructed.

A sample is produced from the 9-dimensional variable space, and t-SNE is applied to produce a 2-dimensional embedding of the space.

Final project for 4.453 Creative Machine Learning for Design, Spring 2020.
